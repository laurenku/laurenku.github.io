Enchanted Objects In Enchanted Objects, I’m interested in examining how technologies exist outside of a focus on efficiency, ergonomics, and simplicity and satisfy some fundamental human desires. Through the trials of reviving a Nabaztag (an ambient electronic device that was released on the market in 2006) I engage with a deliberate lack of functionality. Furthermore, I consider the dominant role of cuteness in consumer society by heightening and compromising the cuteness of my Nabaztag.

This project was supported in part by funding from the Carnegie Mellon University BXA Intercollege Degree Programs.

Title inspired by Enchanted Objects by David L. Rose.
