Eggdog Extravaganza for Emily and Angie I collaborated with Mikey Cao, Matthew Komar, Sarah Di, Joyce Zhang, and Rowan de Boer to create Eggdog Extravaganza, a Visual Novel game. Using the Ren’py game engine, I programmed the gameplay and animations in Python. I also illustrated 2D assets with Photoshop. This game is a birthday gift to our friends Emily Ngo and Angie Orbeta.

To play the game, download the game release from Github. Click on AEParty-1.0-pc.zip under Assets (117 MB). Once the ZIP file downloads, extract the files and click on AEParty.exe.
